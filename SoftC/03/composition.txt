Our faculty aim is to educate and train talented exparts that support
the coming age of Highly Information Technological Society, with our 
professional education research systems from the basic information   
technology theories to interdisciplinary application related areas
in computer and information science.
Our faculty is consisting of four co-related courses, and each course
consists of several laboratories, each with a location space and with
at most of 8 students per academic year for each lab.
